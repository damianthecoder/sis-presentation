Operational riskWhy this matters
The weak link

Cancellations are not a staffing inconvenience. They are supply failure.

The public SIS promise depends on continuous, trusted, low-friction content. Every avoidable cancellation weakens the operator proposition: event volume, betting rhythm, margin consistency and partner confidence.

If the feed is live

Sportsbooks get more betting moments, repeat engagement and reliable markets.

If the feed breaks

Production quality, integrity narrative and commercial value all take the hit.

Therefore

The operating model must be measured like a content supply chain, not managed like casual shift cover.

The pilot is not a bonus trial. It is a reliability system.

Pay, scheduling, progression and cover have to reinforce the same behaviour: show up, perform, recover cleanly, and keep the feed live.
