Cancellation problem
Problem

The current model treats absence, fatigue and weak incentives as separate problems. They compound into cancellation risk.

Failure mode

  • No-show or late drop creates a direct content gap.
  • Flat progression weakens commitment after poor runs.
  • Recovery depends on manual intervention rather than designed cover.

Control lever

  • On-call cover converts many failures into recoverable incidents.
  • Tier movement gives every match a visible consequence.
  • SPS creates an auditable performance signal for progression.
